# Advisory Board: Customer Experience Overview

> **Date:** 2026-04-18
> **Presenter:** Derek (Developer)
> **Topic:** What the Hy-lee website delivers for our customers

## The Core Principle

Everything we've built is designed around one question: what does a customer need to feel confident enough to buy, and valued enough to come back?

---

## What Our Website Does for Customers

### 1. Makes It Easy to Find the Right Product

Customers shopping for small-space living have specific needs. Our website helps them get to the right product quickly — through smart search, category browsing, and filters that show exactly what they've applied and let them adjust without starting over. The goal is zero frustration between landing on the site and finding what they're looking for.

---

### 2. Gives Them the Information to Say Yes

Once a customer finds a product, the page is designed to answer every question before they have to ask — photos, dimensions, specs, and real reviews from other customers. For furniture and appliances specifically, there's a built-in fit-check feature so customers can confirm a product works for their space before buying. The result is fewer hesitations, fewer returns, and more confident purchases.

---

### 3. Gets Them Through Checkout Without Friction

Checkout is where most online stores lose customers. Ours walks them through the process step by step, keeps things simple (one shipping option, no re-entering information), and gives them a full order review before they commit. A clear confirmation page closes the loop. Less friction at this stage directly improves conversion.

---

### 4. Builds a Relationship, Not Just a Transaction

Customers with an account can see their full order history, manage their wishlist and addresses, and track active orders — all in one place. Paired with our promotional tier structure (first purchase, account creation, referrals, partner codes), we have built-in reasons for customers to engage more deeply over time. Stackable discounts reward loyalty without eroding margin on already-discounted products.

---

### 5. Stands Behind Every Sale

Returns are handled through a guided process where customers choose their resolution — exchange, replacement, store credit, or refund. The experience is designed to feel supportive, not adversarial. Customers who have a smooth return are more likely to buy again than customers who never returned anything. Our policies (return policy, FAQ) are clearly written and easy to find, which reduces anxiety before and after purchase.

---

### 6. Ready to Grow With Us

The website is built to support multiple languages and scale without rebuilding from scratch. As the customer base grows, we can expand into new markets without significant additional development investment.

---

## What This Means for the Business

- **Conversion** — removing friction at every step from search to checkout
- **Retention** — accounts and promotions that give customers a reason to come back
- **Lifetime value** — a return experience that turns a potential churn moment into a re-purchase
- **Scale** — a foundation that grows without proportional cost
